About Me


I find Software Development endlessly fascinating.

For a little background, I've spent the previous decade working in and managing kitchens. Around the middle of 2019, it became clear to me that I needed a change in careers and I began to look around. At the behest of some dear friends, I tried writing some code.

Starting with various free online courses, I was immediately hooked. I discovered that I get a huge amount of satisfaction from creating a system that does exactly what it's supposed to do and then tweaking that system to operate more efficiently. After completing a few courses and assessing my progress, it became clear to me that if I wanted to start working in the industry soon I needed a technical boost. I did some research, decided that CodeWorks was the best fit for me and enrolled.

Since then, I've been on a whirlwind of learning and my fascination with Development has only grown. I've discovered that I'm good at organizing teams, managing a workflow, producing functional products and, more than anything else, diving into a problem and staying in it until it is solved.
